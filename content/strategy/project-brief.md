# Project Brief

## Working Title

AI Adoption Blueprint

## Mission

Create a practical website for AI architects and companies adapting to AI. The site should help readers understand how AI adoption changes strategy, organizational design, software architecture, security, delivery, and production operations.

## Primary Promise

Help serious teams move from scattered AI experimentation to measurable, governed, production-grade AI adoption.

## Differentiation

This site should not be another AI news, prompt, or tool-list site. It should focus on operating models, system design, production constraints, security, adoption psychology, and implementation playbooks.

## Content Pillars

1. AI Adoption Operating Manual.
2. AI Adoption Stages.
3. Tools for Building Production-Grade AI Agents.
4. AI Security Best Practices.

## Tone

Direct, practical, candid, and grounded. The writing can be personal and opinionated, but should stay useful and avoid overclaiming.

## Non-Goals

- Do not become a generic AI news site.
- Do not publish private personal notes from `MEMORY.md`.
- Do not create broad claims without citations.
- Do not optimize for hype-heavy social media framing at the expense of technical credibility.
