
## Preamble

The single most important concept on this site has nothing to do with AI or AI adoption. It's a concept to every system in this universe. It's a concept that applies to all progress and change. Slow and steady. The most fundamental progress in the universe shapes through small and steady changes over time. Perhaps it's evolution, God, or the laws of physics; but it's always been in the background. The same concept applies to AI adoption. It’s not about the big leaps, it’s about the small steps, it’s about the consistent atomic wins. It’s not about the big visions, it’s about executing visions. 

AI is possibly the most powerful tool we have ever created. It has the potential to change everything, to disrupt everything, to create everything. But it’s not going to happen overnight. We should approach it with patience and strategy. We should change with it indeed, but change slowly; and change with it, not against it. 

In his book Atomic Habits, James Clear talks about the power of small wins. He says that if you can get 1% better every day, you will be 37 times better in a year. The same applies to AI adoption. The key is to focus on changing the habits, changing out mindset, changing the way we work. Architecting our environment for these changes to be possible. This is not a small change therefore it will not happen over night and it shall not. The key is to focus on the now, not the later. The key is to focus on the small wins, not the big wins.

Focus on improving the process, your habits, your mindset, your environment at the most atomic level every day.

Let this paragraph ground you if you find yourself with revolutionary leaps and visions on how you can use AI in your company or in your life. And of course, always remind yourself as you read the rest of the content on this site, that I'm completely talking out of my ass. No one has any idea what we're going to do!

## The Big Picture

The one single most important thing to understand about AI is that it’s a tool. It’s not a magic wand, it’s not a genie, it’s not a crystal ball. It’s a tool that can be used to augment human intelligence, to automate tasks, to generate content, to analyze data, to make predictions, to solve problems, to create new things. It’s a tool that can be used for good or for bad, depending on how we use it. It’s a tool that can be used by anyone, regardless of their background, their education, their experience, their skills. It’s a tool that can be used by individuals, by teams, by organizations, by societies. It’s a tool with knowledge in any domain, in any industry, in any field. It’s a tool that can be used in any way we can imagine.
